### Connection
Vcc - 3,3V  
TX - RX  
RX - TX  
GND - GND
   
### Additional Information
PowStro are fake Sonoff Basic, but they still work with Tasmota.   
More Infos can be found here: [#1419](https://github.com/arendst/Tasmota/issues/1419).
